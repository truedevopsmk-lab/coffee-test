### Extra Context
Cafes Muda focuses on contemporary specialty coffees with an emphasis on expressive processing and clarity across both filter and espresso. Nestor Lasso, working alongside his brother Adrian and partner Jhoan Vergara at El Diviso in Huila, represents a new generation of Colombian producers who have invested heavily in experimentation and quality improvement. Their coffees are frequently featured in European barista competitions, reflecting a reputation for precision fermentation and vibrant profiles. Natural Sidra lots from El Diviso are typically appreciated for their aromatic intensity, floral lift, and structured sweetness, appealing to drinkers who enjoy layered, competition-style filter coffees.
