**Roaster:** Sonic Beans  
**Origin:** El Salvador  
**Estate / Farm:** Plan del Hoyo  
**Producer:** Francisco R.R. de Sola & family  
**Variety:** Gesha  
**Process:** Washed  
**Roast:** Filter-oriented (light/medium)  
**Altitude:** ~1750 – 1760 m  
**Roasted On:** 18/12/25  

### Flavor Notes (Roaster)
- Cocoa with mellow Kahlua and savory flavors  
- Mild balanced acidity

*(as described on the bag)*

### Extra Context
Plan del Hoyo is a high-elevation coffee farm in the Apaneca-Ilamatepec region of El Salvador, operated by Francisco R.R. de Sola and family, noted in specialty circles for carefully managed, clarity-focused lots. Gesha from this farm typically expresses floral and fruit intensity with a refined, elegant profile, owing to the terroir’s cool mountain climate and meticulous post-harvest handling. Sonic Beans’ release focuses on a washed Gesha that highlights balanced acidity and sweetness, making it suitable for filter brewing and appreciated by drinkers who enjoy delicate floral aromatics and nuanced sweetness without heavy processing influence. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
