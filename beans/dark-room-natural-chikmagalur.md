### Extra Context

Ratnagiri Estate is widely regarded as one of India’s leading specialty coffee producers, known for experimental processing and high-clarity profiles. This lot undergoes anaerobic fermentation followed by extended **dark-room drying**, a method designed to preserve volatile aromatics and sugars by minimizing UV exposure during drying.

In community discussions, this coffee is often praised for delivering **juicy fruit character without excessive funk**, making it appealing even to drinkers who are cautious about heavily fermented naturals. Expect layered tropical fruit sweetness balanced by tea-like structure.
