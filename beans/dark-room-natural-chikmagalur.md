## Dark Room Natural — Chikmagalur, India (Anaerobic Natural)

**Roaster:** Dark Room Natural (DrN)  
**Origin:** Chikmagalur, Karnataka, India  
**Estate / Farm:** Ratnagiri Estate  
**Producer:** Ratnagiri Estate  
**Variety:** Catuai  
**Process:** Anaerobic Natural (Dark Room Drying)  
**Roast:** Light  
**Altitude:** 1300–1350 MASL  
**Roasted On:** 29-12-2025  

### Flavor Notes (Roaster)
- Passionfruit  
- Mosambi  
- Pineapple  
- Black tea  

### Extra Context
Ratnagiri Estate is widely regarded as one of India’s leading specialty coffee producers, known for experimental processing and high-clarity profiles. This lot undergoes anaerobic fermentation followed by extended dark-room drying, a method designed to preserve volatile aromatics and sugars.

In community discussions, this coffee is often praised for delivering juicy fruit character without excessive funk, making it approachable even for those cautious about heavily fermented naturals.
