## Fast Flat-bed Default
- Ratio: 1:15.5
- Temp: 90–94°C
- Pour pattern: 4 pulses
- Brew time: 2:00–2:45

## Notes
- Dripper reference: [TIMEMORE Crystal Eye B75](https://www.timemore.com/products/timemore-crystal-eye-b75-dripper-pctg)
- The fast-flow design can increase clarity; adjust grind finer if drawdown is too quick.
